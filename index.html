about our jewellery

  • All of our jewellery is made with solid gold; we love its radiant colour and shine.
  • Solid gold is known for its resistance to rust, corrosion and hypoallergenic qualities - perfect
    for cartilage earrings which you may change less frequently than those in your lobes.
  • Pure gold (24k gold) is too soft for everyday wear so solid gold jewellery is made
    by alloying other metals with pure gold.
  • We make all of our jewellery with 9k or 14k solid gold, using a mixture of silver.
